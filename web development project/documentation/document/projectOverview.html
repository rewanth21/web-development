PROJECT OVERVIEW

Space facts is a website which provides information about our Solar System. The information includes all the eight planets(Pluto also has been added as a ninth planet for the sake of this website, but it is classified as a dwarf planet and information regarding it is provided in the website) and our star, the Sun, the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter, the Kuiper Belt and beyond our solar system.

The project homepage consists of an animated depiction of our Solar System and brief information about it. Scrolling down will show you what our System contains and clicking on then will expand and collapse the content.

Each individual page gives a descriptive information as to what our star is made of, what each planet is made of, what the asteroid belt is made of and what is beyond our Solar system with images and the top and a brief video at the end of the page showcasing each object.

Apart from that, to the right of the webpage is a list of all the objects in our solar system and hovering over each one which will give a brief description. Clicking on them will open a new page showcasing that particular object.
